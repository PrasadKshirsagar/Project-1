About Blood

Blood cannot be manufactured in factories; it can only come from generous donors.According to WHO data, India faces a shortage of 3 million blood units. This shortage can easily be eliminated if only an additional 2% of India's youth donates blood. To make this possible, BloodConnect acts as a channel connecting voluntary blood donors with those who need blood. It is currently a project under IIT ROPAR which aims organization and provides free help and specially works to target the patient and the needy.
